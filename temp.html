Summary: Latitude Vs. Max Temperature (°F)

It is a well known fact that the closer you are to the equator, 0° latitude, the warmer you can expect the temperature to be. The weather is in fact significantly warmer towards 0 latitude and that temperature combined with feet above sea level can become uncomfortable. Depending on how you prefer your weather, you may choose to vacation at one of the cities closest to 0° latitude, especially if you live anywhere greater than ±20°.

This scatter plot is a visual representation of how the temperature ebbs and flows based on how much the representative city's location gets closer to the equator. At this time of the year, because of the tilt of the earth, cities located below 0 latitude will have the higher temperatures. For additional information please consult either a scientist or a weather forecaster.
